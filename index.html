qpost

A micro post utility

Examples:

qpost get 127.0.0.1:8080/countries
qpost post 127.0.0.1:8080/countries '{"Name": "USA", "Language": "English"}'

qls

A ls clone, but is two times more faster.

Examples:

 root          4096 Mon Feb  7 11:36:42 2022 root
